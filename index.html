Awero's Song

Verse 1

Beneath the moonlit sky, where stars softly gleam,
In the garden of love, where dreams take their theme.
Bukola Awero Omolola, a name so divine,
In the tapestry of my heart, your love does entwine.

Chorus Bukola, oh Bukola, like a gentle breeze,
Your love sweeps through my soul with such ease.
In the dance of our hearts,
a rhythm so sweet,
Bukola Awero Omolola, love's heartbeat.

Verse 2

Your laughter, a melody, like a songbird's tune,
In the warmth of your gaze, beneath the silver moon.
Bukola, your presence, a sunshine ray,
In the darkest of nights, you light my way.

Chorus Bukola, oh Bukola, in the starry night,
Your love, a constellation, shining bright.
In the symphony of our love, a harmonious song,
Bukola Awero Omolola, where we belong.

Bridge In the whispers of the wind, your name is carried,
Bukola, my love for you, forever married.
Through the seasons of joy,
and moments we share,
Bukola Awero Omolola, a love so rare.

Verse 3

With every heartbeat, my love for you grows,
In the garden of passion, where affection flows.
Bukola, a promise written in the sands of time,
Together, forever, our souls intertwined.

Chorus Bukola, oh Bukola, in the sunrise glow,
Our love, a river that continues to flow.
In the canvas of our story, painted so bold,
Bukola Awero Omolola, a love to be told.

Outro As the journey unfolds, hand in hand we stride,
Bukola, my love for you, an eternal guide.
In this love poem song, our hearts serenade,
Bukola Awero Omolola, in love's embrace, we're swayed.
